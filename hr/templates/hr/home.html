About ACT College

The American College of Technology (ACT) is committed to educational excellence. Our integrated Human Resource Management System provides administrative staff, educators, and leaders with the state-of-the-art tools required to handle attendance via secure rotating QR tokens, process payroll efficiently, track leaves, and review KPI compliance.

Role-Based Security

Secure, partitioned access for Employees, HR Managers, and Executive Deans.

Rotating Tokens

Dynamic, rotating QR badges preventing check-in spoofing and duplicate logs.

Attendance-Linked Payroll

Automated salary calculations mapping absent deductions to slips.
